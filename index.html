Origin Story

The Day the
Sky Fell

It came without warning. A meteor — vast, ancient, and carrying something far stranger than rock — reshaped the world in moments. Cities were swallowed by jungle. Rivers changed course overnight. And in the silence that followed, something entirely new began to grow.

You awaken as the lone survivor in a hidden bunker, emerging into an Earth transformed.

Fifty years have passed. The world healed itself in ways no scientist predicted. Lush biomes brim with colours unknown before the impact. Strange creatures roam lands never mapped by human hands. And everywhere — beneath the beauty — anomaly energy lingers in the soil, the water, and in you. Nobody knows what prolonged exposure does. Nobody who found out came back to tell.

Found the old city today. Or what's left of it. Vines as thick as my arm have split the highway clean in half. In the middle of the intersection — a flower I've never seen. Glowing faintly blue. The scanner went haywire near it. I took a sample. I probably shouldn't have.
